## Detective - Summary

Use the Summary page to spot entities involved in unusual activity over the past 24 hours. You can:

- See investigations for potential security events
- Find entities active in new geolocations
- Identify entities making the most API calls
- Spot EC2 instances with the most traffic
- See container clusters with the most containers

Adjust the time window to explore different periods and use visualizations to link activity to GuardDuty findings.
