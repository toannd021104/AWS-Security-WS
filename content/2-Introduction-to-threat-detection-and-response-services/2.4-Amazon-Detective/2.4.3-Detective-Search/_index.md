## Detective - Search

#### Search for an AWS role

1. Open the **Search** page in Detective. https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/detective/home?region=us-east-1#search

2. For **Select type**, choose **AWS Role** and enter "WSParticipantRole". Click **Search**.
   ![VPC](/images/2/2.4/2.4.3/s2.png)

3. Click the **Principal ID** to see how the role has been used. Review the visualizations and tabs for more insights. Note the scope time in the top right corner. Does this look like what you have been doing so far in the workshop? It should!

![VPC](/images/2/2.4/2.4.3/s2b.png)
