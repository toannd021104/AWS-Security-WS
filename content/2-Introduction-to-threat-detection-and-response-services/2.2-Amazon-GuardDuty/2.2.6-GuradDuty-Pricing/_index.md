#### Security Hub - Pricing

1. GuardDuty shows you how many days are left in your free trial and your average daily cost, so you can plan your budget easily.

2. To check usage, open the **Usage** page under Settings.
   ![VPC](/images/2/2.2-Amazon-GuardDuty/2.2.6-GuradDuty-Pricing/s2.png)

3. Review your current trial usage and estimated cost. For more details, see the AWS documentation.
